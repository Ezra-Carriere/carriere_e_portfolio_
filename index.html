About Me

My name is Ezra Carriere

I am a Fanshawe College student in the Interactive Media Design program. Throughout my time in the program, I have learned many different skills in the world of web development. I have worked with different coding languages, many Adobe programs, and plenty of other general web development and design techniques. Learning all these different things, I have found myself to find a passion in design. Designing websites and different components for them like logos and advertising too. I continue to learn different techniques and strategies to improve with my design work both in and outside of school.
Outside of my work I am passionate about many things. I am a big sports fan, primarily soccer and basketball. Both playing and watching sports is easily one of my favourite ways to spend my free time. I also love watching movies. Film has been a very important part of my life for many years now and I only continue to become more obsessed with it.
